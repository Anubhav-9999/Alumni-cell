About Alumni Meet 2026

A celebration of JUIT legacy and future possibilities

Bringing Together The JUIT Family

The JUIT Alumni Meet 2026 is a momentous occasion where alumni from different batches, branches, and career paths come together to celebrate their shared heritage and achievements.

This grand reunion will feature networking sessions, award ceremonies, guest lectures by accomplished alumni, and various recreational activities to strengthen the bonds of the JUIT community.

Whether you graduated in 2008 or 2025, this is your opportunity to reconnect with old friends, mentor current students, and explore opportunities for collaboration and growth.
